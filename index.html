WELCOME TO MEDILINK CLINIC

Medlink Clinic is a 100% black owned and managed
Human Consulting Company based in Pretoria, South Africa.

Our business activities are focused on Human Resources Consulting,
Medico Legal services, Psychometric Assessments, Career Guidance and are not restricted in nature.


Why Choose Medilink?

We are a young company looking to push the boundaries of excellence and committed to delivering exceptional service to its clients. Our clients experience special treatment from our team due to our company being small medium enterprise.

Mission Statement

To offer professional and research based and customised solutions through: Competent team of industry experts. Strict compliance to the guidelines of the HPCSA. Providing timeous and quality reports.

Vision Statement

To grow the Medlink Clinic into a primary healthcare center.

About Us

  • Improve Quality
    We ensure all experts have undertaken Medico-legal Training, with all new experts completing the Bond Solon Medical Expert Training Courses.
  • Ease Communication

    Communicating between Solicitors and Medical Experts can be challenging. Medlink Clinic provides a single point of contact to streamline interaction and ensure clarity.

  • Reduce Cost

    As government legislation tightens and linkage increases, there is a growing need to reduce litigation costs. We remove traditional intermediary fees to make services more cost-effective.

  • Reduce Complexity

    We simplify the process of instructing an expert, making it repeatable and efficient, supported by our bespoke case management system.
